Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and processes.

Who needs to file an Income Tax Return?
Any individual whose income exceeds the basic exemption limit, or who wishes to claim a refund, must file their return. Additionally, filing is mandatory for certain categories regardless of income — such as those holding foreign assets, making high-value transactions, or depositing above specified thresholds.
What documents are required for ITR filing?
The key documents include: PAN card, Aadhaar card, Form 16 (from employer), bank account statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D, etc.), details of any other income (rental, capital gains, etc.), and TDS certificates. Our team will provide you a complete checklist specific to your case.
How do you help with GST compliance?
We assist with timely GST return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, annual returns), error checks and reconciliations, input tax credit optimization, responding to GST notices, and strategic advice to ensure you're always compliant and minimising your GST liability.
Do you provide ongoing tax advisory?
Yes, we provide year-round advisory services. Our team stays connected with you for queries, important deadlines, notices received, and proactive tax planning — so you're never caught off guard.
Can you help with notices received from Income Tax or GST?
Absolutely. Receiving a notice can be stressful, but it's often manageable with the right expertise. We handle all types of income tax and GST notices — scrutiny assessments, demand notices, refund adjustments — and represent you before the authorities.

Do you serve NRIs and businesses outside Delhi?
Yes. While we are based in New Delhi, we serve clients across India and NRIs worldwide through digital document submission and online consultations. Tax filing and advisory is entirely possible remotely with our streamlined process.
